_Distraction is costly._ Try doing 2× 90min deep work sessions. Silence notifications. Use a physical timer, block distractions.

**Rules:**

1. Pick a single task.
2. Set a clear end time.
3. Phone out of sight.
4. Log what you worked on at the end.
