We often **overestimate** what can be achieved in a single heroic session and **underestimate** the power of steady, consistent effort.

> The secret to long-term progress is not intensity, but consistency.

- Show up even when unmotivated.
- Track streaks rather than perfect output.
- Celebrate "never zero" days.

### Tiny Steps Compound

Your best work emerges not in a flash, but after many quiet, regular days.
